Fab Lab Egypt
A Member of Massachusetts Institute of Technology — Fab Lab Global Network

A Non-Profit, Non-Governmental, Community Run Public Makerspace and Digital Fabrication Lab.
Where anyone in the community from young children to students, entrepreneurs and businesses can get together to share knowledge, learn from each other, and collaborate on projects and hands-on activities.

An environment that promotes innovation and education providing a collaborative open makerspace equipped
with digital fabrication and prototyping machines & tools. Hands-on training using MIT accredited curriculum.
